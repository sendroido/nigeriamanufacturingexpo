Conference

Learn from industry leaders sharing their knowledge and experience. Attendance is free of charge for all visitors.
The free-to-attend conference at the Nigerian 2020 aims to create a platform where industry experts can share their knowledge, expertise and experience.

Engr Mansur Ahmed
President, Manufacturers Association of Nigeria

President's Introduction

" The NME Expo is very important for retooling and modernising the Nigerian Manufacturing Industry. "

Why attend?

  • Hear from industry leaders sharing knowledge and best practice
  • Learn capacity building and business management training - up skill your team
  • Gain insights from real-life business case studies
  • Get Finance and Funding Advice from the experts

Highlight

The current and developing environment in which disruptive technologies and trends such as Block chain; Internet of Things; Machine Learning; Artificial Intelligence; Driverless car and Augmented Realty, among others, would dictate the pace of development among nations. Therefore, the need to start using technology to produce our own food, products and other indigenous innovations becomes imperative so that we do not end dependent on developed countries.
